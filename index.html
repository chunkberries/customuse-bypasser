How to Use

Follow these steps to get your design files.

1

Find your Design

Go to Customuse. Select a design you like. Click the three dots (options menu) and select "Edit Info" or "Report".

2

Copy the Link

In the browser address bar or the share modal, copy the full URL. It usually looks like customuse.com/d/123456.

3

Paste & Download

Paste the link into the tool on the homepage. Click "Fetch Assets" and download your transparent PNG files.

Note: This tool works best for shirt/pants templates. 3D accessories are not supported.
